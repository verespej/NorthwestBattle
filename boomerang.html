The average American household has $7,000 of unwanted stuff - this represents over $800 Billion nationwide. There are plenty of ways to sell it on your own- Craigslist, Yard Sales, etc. But, these methods tend to be huge hassles and potentially even dangerous. Because it is such a pain to sell stuff, many people end up just throwing away things that have real value. Or, people accept far less than their stuff is worth- just because they want to get rid of it. The situation becomes even worse if you are moving, or perhaps helping parents downsize homes. Then, you’re trying to sell a lot of stuff - quickly. Even people who are ok with selling one or two things shudder at the thought of trying to sell dozens or hundreds of things simultaneously. Boomerang takes the pain out of turning unwanted stuff into cash because it is a professional full service solution. And because Boomerang is exclusively in the business of selling used stuff, we are efficient, knowledgeable about market value and selling strategies and are thus able to get top dollar. Boomerang makes money via a share of the revenue we generate by selling people’s stuff.
