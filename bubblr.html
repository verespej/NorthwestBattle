Music Bubble was recently awarded both "Fan Favorite" and "Best Executed" at Portland Startup Weekend. Since Startup Weekend, Music Bubble has undergone a name change and are ready to show more of our exciting application. There is currently a disconnection between what we see as three important points in music distribution: local artists, local venues, and most importantly, local listeners. bubblr is a music service designed to solve this disconnect. bubblr uses a simple idea of bubbles to share music with users in a hyper local manner. Bubbles make sharing and discovering music both easy and fun.The process starts with an artist dropping a bubble centered on their current location, with a radius being their initial coverage on a map interface. A bubble is essentially a track in geospace. If a listener is within range to an artist’s bubble, the track is queued to the listeners playlist. The listener is then able to upvote a track to increase the bubbles coverage or downvote a track to diminish the coverage. Bubbles also provide a way for users to discover small businesses, which can also share their own special bubbles. For example, a music venue can use their bubbles to feature upcoming shows/artists and perform in app ticket sales. An innumerable variety of small businesses can reach out to their customers using a granular marketing scheme purchasing bubbles in different pay tiers. Businesses succeed through this model, because bubblr is based on providing users with a seamless audio experience that is visually stimulating, engaging, and enlightening. bubblr allows users to spend less time on their device and more time sharing and discovering the music they love.
