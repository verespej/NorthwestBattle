Millions of tablets are going into classrooms each year, but most teachers don’t have the time in their busy schedules to find all new digital lessons. At the same time, many teachers have a well developed set of worksheets they use in their classrooms. With MyWorksheets app, teachers can quickly and easily transform their existing worksheets into digital lessons delivered on their students’ tablets. This easily adoptable platform immediately gives teachers access to the benefits of technology including real time data on student progress, easy access to student work, and features such as rewarding students with points. Starting with worksheets, MyWorksheets will become the foundation on which teachers will be able to transition to fully interactive digital lessons through future integration of interactive learning apps and customized learning tools.
