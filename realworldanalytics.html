My mother used to own a retail store and it's a hassle to keep up with all your customers, your inventory, and your staff and to have statistics about your store at the same time. Who came in? Did my marketing strategy work? How many of my costumers are new costumers or returning customers? What a painful process to look through point of sales data to find the answers to these questions. Actually, it's impossible find the answers to some of these questions from register data alone. Wouldn't it be great for business owners to be able to answer these questions about their customers?Marketing strategies can be modified, operational efficiency can be increased, and in store organization can be optimized. That is what real world Analytics can do. Using a simple plug and play product, data can be generated fork incoming customers based on passive anonymous wifi data and visualized on a private web server. Other competitors exist in the market but this is only market validation for us as they are targeting bigger fish. Real World Analytics will focus on small to medium size franchises or businesses.
